How It Works

  • Basic Salary: The base annual salary for the job.
  • Employer Pension Contribution: Calculated as [(Basic Salary + Annual Bonus) * Employer Pension Rate / 100].
  • Annual Leave Value: Calculated as [(Basic Salary + Annual Bonus) / (260 workdays - Annual Leave Days)] * Annual Leave Days.
  • Parental Leave Value: Calculated as [(Basic Salary + Annual Bonus) / (52 workweeks - Parental Leave Weeks)] * Parental Leave Weeks.
  • 4-Day Week Value: For jobs offering a 4-day workweek, the value is calculated as (Basic Salary + Annual Bonus) / (260 workdays - (52 * 1)) * (52 * 1).
