How It Works

  • Basic Salary: The base annual salary for the job.
  • Employer Pension Contribution: Calculated as (Basic Salary * Employer Pension Rate / 100).
  • Annual Leave Value: Calculated as (Basic Salary / 260 workdays) * Annual Leave Days.
  • Parental Leave Value: Calculated as (Basic Salary / 52 workweeks) * Parental Leave Weeks.
  • 4-Day Week Value: For jobs offering a 4-day workweek, the value is calculated as (Basic Salary / 260 workdays) * 52 days.
